About Us

Pawsitiverescues0612 is the result of a deep-rooted love for animals and a burning desire to make a positive impact on their lives. Founded by Suhani Sharma and Kashish Sharma, with a passion for animal welfare.We are not an organization.

Mission and Values

Mission -

Our mission is simple yet powerful - to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals in distress. We believe that every animal deserves a chance at a happy and healthy life, regardless of their past circumstances. We strive to create a compassionate community that advocates for the well-being of all living beings.

Values -

1. Compassion   : Every action is guided by a deep sense of empathy and kindness.
2. Responsibility: We take responsibility for the animals in our care, ensuring their physical and emotional
                               well-being
3. Transparency : We believe in open communication and sharing our journey with our supporters.
4. Community    : Building a community that cares for animals is at the heart of our work.

Services

What We Do

1.Rescue             : We rescue animals from the streets, and challenging situations, providing them with a safe
                               haven.
2.Rehabilitation : Our team works tirelessly to rehabilitate animals, addressing their physical and emotional needs.
3.Rehoming        : We carefully match animals with loving families, ensuring they find forever homes where they can
                               thrive.
4.Feeding Drives: We understand the importance of providing nourishment to our furry friends. In addition to
                               rescue and rehoming, we actively engage in feeding programs for the local stray dogs and cats.
                               This is our way of ensuring that no animal goes hungry in our community.
